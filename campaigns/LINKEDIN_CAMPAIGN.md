## 1. LinkedIn Account Limits & Safety Rules

### 1.1 Rate Limits by Account Type

|--------|--------------|-----------------|----------------|
| Connection requests | ~100/week | ~100/week | **20/day** |

| Profile views | ~500/day | ~1,000/day | **80/day** |
| Search results | ~100/month | Unlimited | N/A |
| Pending invitations | ~700 max | ~700 max | Withdraw after 14 days |

### 1.2 Anti-Spam Golden Rules

1. **Never exceed 20 connection requests per day** ‚Äî LinkedIn's SSI (Social Selling Index) drops fast with mass requests
2. **Personalize every connection request** ‚Äî Generic requests get flagged. Always use a note (300 char max)
3. **Never pitch in the connection request** ‚Äî Connection requests with sales language get reported
4. **Acceptance rate must stay above 40%** ‚Äî Below 30% triggers LinkedIn restrictions
5. **Withdraw pending requests after 14 days** ‚Äî Unaccepted requests hurt your account health
6. **Mix activities** ‚Äî Don't just send requests. Like posts, comment, share. Be a real person
7. **Warm-up period** ‚Äî New or inactive accounts: Start with 5 requests/day for 2 weeks
8. **No automation tools that violate LinkedIn TOS** ‚Äî Dux-Soup, LinkedIn Helper, etc. risk permanent bans
9. **Use LinkedIn's native scheduling** where available
10. **Breaks between sessions** ‚Äî Don't blast 20 requests in 5 minutes. Spread over hours

## 11. Legal & Compliance Notes

### LinkedIn Terms of Service
- ‚úÖ **Personalized messages:** OK
- ‚úÖ **Sales Navigator InMail:** OK (it's what it's designed for)
- ‚ùå **Automated sending tools:** Violation of TOS ‚Äî risk permanent ban
- ‚ùå **Scraping profiles:** Violation of TOS
- ‚úÖ **Manual outreach with templates:** OK (as long as personalized)

### CAN-SPAM (doesn't apply to LinkedIn)
LinkedIn messages are not email and are not subject to CAN-SPAM. However, LinkedIn has its own anti-spam policies. Be professional and respect "not interested" responses immediately.

### GDPR (if targeting EU contacts)
Most E-Rate contacts are US-based (USAC is a US program). If you encounter EU contacts, ensure you have a lawful basis for processing (legitimate interest for B2B outreach is generally accepted).
